High and Tight Crew Cut

What to ask your barber:

  • High fade on the sides and back
  • Short length on top, around 1-2 inches
  • Blended seamlessly for a sharp look

Products to buy:

  • American Crew Fiber - Provides high hold with low shine.
  • Layrite Superhold Pomade - For a stronger hold and a clean finish.

Step-by-step styling:

  1. Apply a small amount of styling product to damp hair.
  2. Use your fingers or a comb to distribute the product evenly.
  3. Blow-dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and shape the top.
  4. Finish with a bit more product to add definition and hold.
