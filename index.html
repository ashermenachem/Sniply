High and Tight Crew Cut

What to ask your barber:

  • High fade on the sides and back.
  • Short length on top, around 1-2 inches.
  • Seamlessly blended for a sharp look.

Products to buy:

  • American Crew Fiber - High hold, low shine.
  • Layrite Superhold Pomade - Strong hold, clean finish.

Step-by-step styling:

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly and towel dry until damp.
  2. Apply a small amount of American Crew Fiber to your fingertips.
  3. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly.
  4. Work the product through your hair, starting from the back and moving to the front.
  5. Use a comb to style your hair upward and slightly to the side, giving it volume.
  6. Blow-dry your hair on a medium heat setting while using your fingers or a comb to lift and shape the top.
  7. Once dry, apply a small amount of Layrite Superhold Pomade for extra hold and definition.
  8. Finish by using a comb or your fingers to finalize the style, ensuring a sharp and clean look.

Faux Hawk

What to ask your barber:

  • Short sides and back with a gradual fade.
  • Longer hair on top, around 3-4 inches.
  • Blend the top into the sides for a smooth transition.

Products to buy:

  • Garnier Fructis Style Power Gel - Strong hold, long-lasting.
  • AXE Messy Look Hair Paste - Medium hold, natural finish.

Step-by-step styling:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of Garnier Fructis Style Power Gel to your hands.
  3. Rub your hands together to warm up the gel.
  4. Work the gel through your hair, focusing on the top section.
  5. Use your fingers to shape the top hair into a faux hawk, lifting it upwards and towards the center.
  6. Blow-dry your hair on a medium heat setting to set the style.
  7. For a more textured look, apply a small amount of AXE Messy Look Hair Paste to the tips of your hair.
  8. Use your fingers to add definition and separate the strands for a messy, natural finish.

Textured Crop

What to ask your barber:

  • Short, blunt fringe at the front.
  • Textured layers throughout the top.
  • Faded sides and back for contrast.

Products to buy:

  • Hanz de Fuko Claymation - High hold, matte finish.
  • Got2b Ultra Glued Invincible Styling Gel - Strong hold, high shine.

Step-by-step styling:

  1. Start with towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of Hanz de Fuko Claymation to your hands.
  3. Rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly.
  4. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  5. Use your fingers to create texture by twisting small sections of hair.
  6. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting while continuing to twist sections for added texture.
  7. Finish by applying a small amount of Got2b Ultra Glued Invincible Styling Gel to the fringe for definition.
  8. Use your fingers to position the fringe forward and slightly to the side.

Comb-Over

What to ask your barber:

  • Longer hair on top, around 4-5 inches.
  • Short sides and back with a clean fade.
  • Defined part on one side.

Products to buy:

  • Baxter of California Clay Pomade - Strong hold, matte finish.
  • Suavecito Pomade Original Hold - Medium hold, high shine.

Step-by-step styling:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of Baxter of California Clay Pomade to your hands.
  3. Rub your hands together to warm up the pomade.
  4. Work the pomade through your hair, focusing on the top section.
  5. Use a comb to create a defined part on one side.
  6. Comb the hair on top over to the opposite side of the part.
  7. Blow-dry your hair on a medium heat setting, using the comb to maintain the part and shape.
  8. Apply a small amount of Suavecito Pomade Original Hold to the top for added shine and hold.
  9. Finish by using the comb to ensure the hair is sleek and well-positioned
  10. Finish by using the comb to ensure the hair is sleek and well-positioned, maintaining the defined part.
