Textured Fringe

What to ask your barber:

  • A short cut with plenty of texture
  • Keep the fringe longer and choppy
  • Thinned out on top to reduce bulk

Products to buy:

  • Product 1
  • Product 2

Step-by-step styling:

  1. Apply a small amount of styling product to damp hair.
  2. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, focusing on the fringe.
  3. Blow-dry while tousling the hair to create texture.
  4. Finish with a matte styling product for hold and definition.
